Privacy Policy

Bear Ride: City Cruise does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

The game integrates Apple's Game Center service to provide leaderboards and other gameplay features. When using Game Center, Apple may collect and process data such as your Game Center nickname, player identifier, and leaderboard scores. This information is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple's own privacy policies and terms.

Bear Ride: City Cruise does not have access to your Apple account information and does not store Game Center data outside of the services provided by Apple.

For more information about how Apple handles data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy
