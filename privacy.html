Bear Ride: City Cruise

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Bear Ride: City Cruise does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

Game Center

The game integrates Apple's Game Center service to provide leaderboards and other gameplay features. When using Game Center, Apple may collect and process information such as your Game Center nickname, player identifier, and leaderboard scores.

This data is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple’s own privacy policies. Bear Ride: City Cruise does not have access to your Apple account information and does not store Game Center data outside of Apple's services.
